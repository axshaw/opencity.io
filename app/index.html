What is it?

As part of the redevelopment of Long Causeway a new interactive display screen will be position in a central location. The opencity project hopes to bring together local businesses and local proffessionals to implment a world first in community delivery of innovation for the city of Peterborough and its residents


Open City is about a groundswell of creative, digital and ideas people coming together to think differently about specific challenges to find new approaches and new solutions. Part of Peterborough DNA, Open City is all about making Peterborough a better place to live and work.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I get involved ?

Simple, register on eventbrite (below) to attend the opening inception at the Town Hall on 1st October 2014

What kind of skills do we need ?

Its important that we have a cross section of people from around the city. Businesses and local heritage sites will be able to use the screen to promote their businesses and events. Designers to help make sure the information presented in a clear and informative way, developers who want to learn new technology and get involved with working alongside other engineers to create a working solution, and finally people who will use the screen when they are in the city to let us know what kind of information would be useful and add value to their shopping experiance.

Who is paying for the event ?

The event is being organised by Peterborough DNA. Hardware and support costs will be funded by the Peterborough DNA scheme in support of innovation and creativity accross the city

Will I get paid for my time ?

No. This event is a community event, a chance to offer local people an opportunity to delivery cutting edge, innovative technology for the promotion and growth of businesses around the city. An opportunity to get involved and be proud of our city.
